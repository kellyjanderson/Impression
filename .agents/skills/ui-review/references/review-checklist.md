# UI Review Checklist

## Findings to Look For

* unstable layout across states
* controls that change meaning silently
* weak or missing feedback
* inaccessible contrast or focus
* poor scanability
* hidden recovery paths
* ugly-data breakage
* clipped text, wrapped-text overflow, or controls cut off by their containers

## Severity Heuristic

* High: breaks task completion, hides critical state, or causes serious accessibility failure
* Medium: likely confusion, misread semantics, or degraded task flow
* Low: polish, clarity, or consistency issue without strong task risk
