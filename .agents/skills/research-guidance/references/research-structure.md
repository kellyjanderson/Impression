# Research Structure

## Goal

Capture durable knowledge that future work can reuse without redoing the research.

## Recommended Document Shape

* `# Topic`
* `## Findings`
* `## Implications`
* `## References`

## Rules

* Prefer durable facts over trend commentary.
* Preserve provenance for later review.
* Separate findings from project decisions.
* Organize by topic, not by date or chat history.
